About

Acme Business Directory

This directory is for businesses who which to find other firms and also for consumers who seeking a particular service or product.

It is free to register your company and free to search for businesses.

There are many categories to search within. Users can either perform a wide general search or search a particular category or area.

If you would like your business to have a more significant listing, you can pay for a premium listing in the directory.

Premium listings

A premium listing entails:

  • your business is placed at the top of the page ensuring it is the first seen;
  • your listing is noticebly larger than a normal listing;
  • you can add more images to the listing.

If you are interested in having a premium listing, please contact us.
